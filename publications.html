Manuscripts Under Review

SBEA: Exploiting Set Bias in Entity Alignment for Label Inference and Model Extraction in Vertical Federated Learning Under Review
F. Mubashwera, et al. — Submitted to IJCAI 2026.
We propose SBEA, a novel attack that exploits set-level biases introduced during entity alignment in VFL to infer private labels and extract model representations from semi-honest participants. We further evaluate candidate defences and quantify the privacy-utility trade-off.

Understanding Gendered Stereotype in Large Language Models: A Mini Review on Biasing and Debiasing Techniques Under Review
F. Mubashwera, et al. — Submitted to Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence.
A systematic mini-review of how gender stereotypes are encoded in contemporary LLMs and the landscape of techniques proposed to detect, measure, and mitigate them. Covers both fine-tuning and prompting-based approaches.
